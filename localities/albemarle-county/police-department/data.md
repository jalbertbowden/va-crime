## Police Districts  

### Blue Ridge District  
[Blue Ridge District](http://www.albemarle.org/department.asp?department=police&relpage=14268)  
[Blue Ridge District Map](http://www.albemarle.org/upload/images/Pictures/Departments/Police/pictures/BRwebpage10-24-16.png)  
The Blue Ridge District comprises the Western and Southern portions of Albemarle County.  The diverse communities, scenic highways and beautiful mountain vistas of Afton, Whitehall, Crozet, Ivy, Batesville, Southwood, Monticello, Keene, Woodridge, Scottsville and Howardsville comprise the 515 square miles that make up this district.  
[Crime Prevention in the Blue Ridge District](http://www.albemarle.org/department.asp?department=police&relpage=20425)
