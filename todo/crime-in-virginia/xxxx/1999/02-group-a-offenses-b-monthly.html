Monthly Group A Offense Categories

  • Assaults include aggravated and simple assaults, and intimidation.
  • Homicide includes, murder/nonnegligent manslaughter and negligent manslaughter.
  • Forcible Sex includes rape, sodomy, sexual assault with object, and forcible fondling.
  • Nonforcible Sex includes incest and statutory rape.

These crime counts represent offenses reported in an incident, not victims of each offense.
